Farah and Kartik

Farah and Kartik's platform is a rather nontraditional, comprehensive Student Body Strategic Plan, encompassing the five main goals of the University's 2020 Strategic Plan in very comparable language. We hope to expand SGA's presence on campus and incorporate the decisions and desires of the student body into the actions of student government. This establishes an expandable resource for each and every student at WVU to incorporate aspects of their daily lives into our representative body. In addition, a sixth goal is added which increases the scope to fit the role of WVU Student Government, to best express the concerns of students on campus.



Farah Famouri



Farah is a Senior International Studies and Philosophy major with a minor in Law and Legal Studies. A Morgantown native, Farah is the Chief of Operations of Student Government Association, after being Director of Student Organizations last year. She is currently President of Mountaineers for International Disease Awareness, a public health student org, and WVU Anarchappella, an acappella group geared toward non-vocal majors. She works as a Philosophy Department tutor. She is also Parliamentarian and Advocacy Chair of WVU Young Democrats and a member of Mortar Board honorary and the Honors College.



Kartik Motwani



Kartik is a B.S. Chemistry major and Leadership Studies minor from Hurricane, WV. He is a South Charleston High School graduate, has served on the Student Government Association Board of Governors and is currently Director of Academics. With commitments in many walks of campus life, he is involved in the athletic tutoring program at the Coliseum and is a member of the Honors college and WVU's American Chemical Society chapter. Kartik also holds eminent positions of public service, in the pre-health society Alpha Epsilon Delta, and vice presidency in both the Undergraduate Neuroscience club and Chimes junior honorary.
