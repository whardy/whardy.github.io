Effy Fuchuruddin

International Student Issues





Effy is from Borneo, Indonesia and is a sophomore international studies major with a business administration and woman and gender studies minor. She was just elected vice president and founding members of WVU Professional Sales Club. Effy was elected 4 years in a row to be "save-a-teen" ambassador for Sampoerna foundation, a program designed to help high-achiving underprivileged young Indonesian to afford basic 12 years of education( at least until graduate from high school).
