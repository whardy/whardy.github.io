Our Strategic Plan



Our Strategic Plan

Engage All Students

In the aspects of college affordability and matters of tuition, wider representation in Faculty Senate and President's Office committees, additional involvement of graduate and professional students, and improvement of transitional programs such as the First Year Seminar, etc.

Nurture Academics and Research

The expansion of undergraduate research opportunities, renovating the GEC and associated curricular requirements, improving student-faculty feedback mechanisms and quality of courses overall.

Inclusivity and Diversity

Broadcasting and supporting cultural student organizations on campus, reevaluating and improving minority group (CBCR, accessibilty, etc.) services, and continuing advocation for centers for culture on campus.

Reach Abroad

An increase of international student outreach and programs similar to mentoring, improving capabilities for majors like Engineering to study abroad, and creating a program to connect current international students and students who plan to study abroad.

Campus Improvement

Addressing existing residential hall issues (air conditioning, international student housing over breaks, healthy dining options, etc), convenience between Evansdale, Downtown, and Health Sciences for all students to incorporate and tie together the campuses, holding a seasonal Safety Walk to ensure campus safety, supporting and expanding sexual assault prevention programs such as GreenDot, improving accessibility problems for students regarding campus transportation, clarifying and adapting issues regarding the campus Tobacco Ban.

Reach West Virginia

Advocating for rural/disadvantaged WV regions, support for programs that give back to West Virginia and the Appalachian region (like WV Uncovered), involvement of all campuses including Potomac State College and WVU Institute of Technology.
